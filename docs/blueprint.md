# **App Name**: River Business

## Core Features:

- Consumption Monitoring: Track water consumption in gallons and liters for each user.
- Water Quality and Sanitation: Provide visibility on water quality compliance reports with attachment view, including sanitation visit reports and visibility through attachments. Manage scheduling and records of sanitation visits with notifications and reports.
- Delivery Tracking: Monitor water deliveries (gallons/liters) to ensure timely supply. Allow the user to attach files to deliveries.
- Predictive Usage Tool: Generative AI tool to predict future water consumption based on past usage patterns, external conditions, and seasonal data. Considers user-defined goals for consumption, with feedback loops to encourage reduction in water consumption
- Customer Service Access: Enable users to easily contact customer service through the app.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#ADD8E6) to evoke a sense of cleanliness and water.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F8FF) to provide a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft teal (#70DBD1) to symbolize environmental consciousness and compliance.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern and readable sans-serif font, for clear presentation of data.
- Use water-themed icons and minimalist, easily recognizable symbols for consumption, delivery, and compliance.
- Dashboard layout: Organize key metrics (consumption, deliveries) prominently. Tabbed interface for detailed views on quality and sanitation. Data visualization with charts.
- Subtle transition animations for data updates.
